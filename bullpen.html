What the signals mean

Every refresh, the QC_Bullpen_Refresh workflow scores each name and writes one of these to the Buy Signal column. A stock can carry more than one (joined with "·"). Priority for action runs top to bottom:

SignalMeaningExact rule
AT TARGET Price has reached your buy zone. Strongest actionable signal — your rating × this signal is the trade trigger. price ≤ Target Entry (your column F)
NEAR +x% Price is approaching your buy zone; x shows how far above your target it sits. price ≤ Target Entry × 1.05 (within 5% above)
WASHOUT Oversold capitulation — potential value entry on quality names, but confirm the thesis first; falling knives also print this. RSI ≤ 30
TURN n/4 Momentum turning back up. 4/4 = all four checks confirm an uptrend; fires at 3 of 4 or better. Count of the four checks below; shows when ≥ 3 pass
— (no target) No signal firing. AT TARGET and NEAR can't evaluate until you set a Target Entry for that row. —

The four TURN checks

  1. Above the 200-day — price > MA200. The long-term trend line is reclaimed.
  2. MACD bullish — MACD trend = bullish. Short-term momentum has crossed up.
  3. RSI in the healthy zone — 50 ≤ RSI ≤ 70. Recovered from oversold without being overbought.
  4. Positive 10-day momentum — 10-day change > 0. Price is actually moving up, not just basing.

This is the "trend-turn" logic from the Cramer chart discussion: 200-DMA reclaim + MACD crossover + RSI recovery + positive momentum. The sheet shows the 5-day column for a quick read, but the TURN check itself uses the 10-day change.

How Score is computed (column I)

Score is the 5-factor composite from the July 2026 pen rescore, out of 100:

FactorWeightWhat it measures
Rule #1 quality25Moat, ROIC, balance sheet, Big-5 growth consistency
Margin of safety20Discount to value — drawdown vs. intrinsic worth, analyst PT gap
Sector thesis20Fit with the AI-infra / space / quantum / drones / robotics theses
Catalyst strength20Nearness and credibility of the thing that re-rates it
Timing15Technical setup — where in the drawdown/recovery it sits

≥65 made the core cut (plus 9 frontier specs kept below that line). Score ranks conviction in the business + setup; the Buy Signal tells you whether today's price is an entry. The actionable cross is your Rating × Signal — e.g. Strong Buy + AT TARGET floats to the top of the Daily Brief rail.

What survives a refresh

Columns E–G (My Rating, Target Entry, My Notes) are yours and persist across refreshes. Everything else — thesis, score, price, technicals, flags, Buy Signal — is workflow-owned and rewritten daily at ~4:45 PM MT and on demand from the Refresh button.
